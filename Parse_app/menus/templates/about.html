JAuNT Link Technologies is a recently established company

In spring 2022, five individuals with backgrounds in education, software development, and business founded JAuNT Link Technologies to transform education through technology. Their mission was to create an innovative Learning Management System (LMS) that would change how educators and learners interact with educational content.

In 2023, JAuNT Link Technologies conducted thorough market research to understand the needs of educators, schools, and businesses. This research guided the development of a comprehensive set of LMS features aimed at addressing the evolving demands of the education sector.

Misson

"To empower educators, learners, and organizations worldwide through innovative technology solutions that enhance the quality of education, foster continuous learning, and bridge the gap between knowledge and achievement."

Vision

"Our vision is to revolutionize the education landscape by creating cutting-edge Learning Management Systems and educational technologies that inspire a lifelong love for learning. We aspire to be the leading catalyst for educational transformation, providing accessible, inclusive, and personalized learning experiences for all."
